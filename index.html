Simplicity First

Setup in seconds.
Literally.

Throw away complicated manuals. No messy IP configurations, no wires. Install Kammy on any two Android devices to create an instant, secure connection.

1

Device A: Camera

Set your spare phone up facing what matters most and select Camera Mode.

2

Device B: Viewer

Use your primary phone, tap Viewer Mode to generate a secure QR code.

3

Scan & Connect

Point the camera device at the QR code. Crystal clear video starts streaming instantly.
