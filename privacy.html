Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Kammy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kammy mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile information (name, email, profile photo).

Camera & Microphone Data

Kammy accesses your device's camera and microphone solely to provide the live streaming and sound detection features you enable. Video and audio streams are transmitted in real-time via encrypted peer-to-peer (WebRTC) connections between your devices. We do not record, store, or process your video or audio streams on our servers.

Device Information

We collect device identifiers (device model, a locally generated UUID) to enable device pairing and remote control features.

Alert Data

When sound alerts are triggered, a snapshot image may be captured and stored locally on your device. Alert metadata (timestamp, sound level) is stored locally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Device Pairing: To connect your devices for remote monitoring and control.
  • Signaling: Our server relays connection setup messages (WebRTC signaling) between your devices. These messages contain session IDs and connection metadata — not video or audio content.
  • TURN Relay: When direct peer-to-peer connections cannot be established, media may be relayed through our TURN server. This data is encrypted and not stored.
  • Crash Reporting: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication. Camera profiles and device registrations are stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that restrict access to your account only. All network communication uses TLS encryption. Video/audio streams use SRTP encryption via WebRTC.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, data storage, and crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (if you subscribe to premium features).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & Export: You can view your data within the app at any time.
  • Delete Account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can revoke camera/microphone permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Kammy is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support.keralogic@gmail.com.
